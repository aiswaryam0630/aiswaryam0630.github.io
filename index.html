Our Mission

The Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACTS) is a student-led organization dedicated to providing students with opportunities for technical growth, professional development, and community engagement.

We aim to bridge the gap between academia and industry by organizing workshops, seminars, coding competitions, and networking events.
